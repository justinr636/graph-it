Selecting a Data File

Click on 'Choose File' and select a .CSV file from your computer.

Choosing a Chart Type

You have the option of creating a Funnel Chart or a Control Chart by choosing an item from the highlighted dropdown menu.

Selecting Labels (or Groups)

Select the column which contains the X Axis Labels.

You can choose to show data for all items in the X Axis, or just the ones you specify.


The same selection method applies for sorting data into groups

Setting the Chart Title and Axis Labels

Change the first highlighted input to set your Chart Title

The next two highlighted inputs will set the X- and Y-Axis labels, respectively.

Creating the Charts

Click the large blue button to create the chart!

Tool Tips

Control Charts

The mean is the green center line.

The control limits (dashed lines) are 3σ away from the mean.

Data points that fall outside the control limits are colored red.


Placing your cursor over any data point or center line will reveal a tooltip

Funnel Plots

The mean is the green center line.

The inner control limits (with long dashed lines) represent the 99.9 % confidence interval.

The outer control limits (with short dashed lines)represent the 95 % confidence interval.


Placing your cursor over any data point or center line will reveal a tooltip

Tool Tips

Placing your cursor over any data point or center line will reveal additional data for that point.

How do I print a chart to a PDF?

Click the print buttons on the top right.

In Google Chrome, change your Printer Destination to 'Save as PDF'

In Mozilla FireFox, click the PDF drop down on the bottom left, and then select 'Save as PDF...'
